02 — Targeting Matrix

Three tracks.
Three buyer mindsets.

Each URL track targets a different stage of the buyer journey. Same infrastructure, different intent, different conversion path.

/build/{industry}/{tool}         → "Build Custom {Tool} for {Industry}"
/best/{industry}/{tool}          → "Best {Tool} App for {Industry}"
/alternatives/{competitor}/...   → "{Competitor} Alternatives for {Industry}"
/build/
Informational
Use-case landing page. Features, benefits, live demo. Searcher is aware of the problem, exploring solutions.
/best/
Commercial
Ranked listicle with pros, cons, pricing. Searcher is evaluating options, not yet committed.
/alternatives/
Transactional
Head-to-head. Competitor vs alternatives, Anything as the final showdown. Wallet already open.

05 — URL Architecture

Four layers.
Infinite scale.

Each layer is a pillar page linking down to children. Internal linking flows naturally from broad to specific, maximising crawl equity at every level.

anything.com/build/
└── {industry}/              Pillar: "Build Custom Apps for {Industry}"
    └── {tool}/              Leaf:   "Build Custom {Tool} for {Industry}"

anything.com/best/
└── {industry}/              Pillar: "Best Apps for {Industry}"
    └── {tool}/              Leaf:   "Best {Tool} App for {Industry}"

anything.com/alternatives/
└── {competitor}/            Pillar: "{Competitor} Alternatives"
    └── {industry}/          Pillar: "... for {Industry}"
        └── {tool}/          Leaf:   "... for {Tool} in {Industry}"
